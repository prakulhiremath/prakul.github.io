BIOLOOP

Overview

BIOLOOP is an algae-based carbon capture and fuel conversion system. The project integrates cultivation, lipid extraction, and downstream fuel pathways into a closed-loop architecture.

Research Domain

Energy Systems → Carbon Capture → Biofuel Conversion

System Architecture

• Photobioreactor cultivation • Carbon feed integration • Biomass harvesting • Lipid extraction • Fuel synthesis

Status

Concept design and system modeling in progress.
