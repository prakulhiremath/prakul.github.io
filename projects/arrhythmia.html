Arrhythmia Modeling

Overview

This project focuses on computational modeling and classification of cardiac rhythm abnormalities using ECG time-series data.

Research Domain

Medical Systems → Cardiac Signal Processing → Time-Series Modeling

Objective

Develop robust models capable of detecting and classifying arrhythmic patterns under noisy and variable clinical conditions.

Methodology

• ECG signal preprocessing and denoising
• Feature extraction (RR intervals, morphology features)
• Temporal modeling (sequence-based learning)
• Probabilistic and Bayesian classification frameworks
• Performance validation on benchmark datasets

Modeling Focus

Emphasis on temporal structure, uncertainty quantification, and interpretability of decision boundaries in medical settings.

Status

Early-stage modeling and dataset evaluation in progress.

Future Work

• Real-time detection pipeline
• Multi-lead integration
• Cross-patient generalization studies
• Clinical validation pathway
